About Adani Power Ltd
BSE: 553309 NSE: ADANIPOWER ISIN: IN814H01011

Adani Power Ltd is in the Power Generation & Distribution sector, having a market capitalization of Rs. 54,344.27 crores. It has reported a consolidated sales of Rs. 7,764.41 crores and a net profit of Rs. 8.77 crores for the quarter ended December 2022. The company management includes Rajesh S Adani (Non Executive Director), Gautam S Adani (Chairman), Deepak Pandya (Company Secretary), Mukesh Shah (Independent Director), Anil Sardana (Managing Director) among others.
