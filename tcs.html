About Tata Consultancy Services Ltd
BSE: 532540 NSE: TCS ISIN: INE467B01029

Company Website: tcs.com

Tata Consultancy Services Ltd is in the IT - Software sector, having a market capitalization of Rs. 1,300,408.56 crores. It has reported a consolidated sales of Rs. 58,229 crores and a net profit of Rs. 10,846 crores for the quarter ended December 2022. The company management includes N Chandrasekaran (Chairman (Non-Executive)),O P Bhatt(Non Exec. And Independent Dir.), Aarthi Subramaniam (Non-exec And Non-Independent Dir.),Rajesh Gopinathan (Managing Director And CEO), N Ganapathy Subramanium (Executive Director And COO) among others.
